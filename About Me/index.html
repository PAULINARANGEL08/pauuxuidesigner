Hello, I am Paulina Rangel.

An enthusiastic, driven, and skilled designer who believes design can impact fundamental elements of digital products as well as delivering a lasting, positive impression on the targeted audience.

I am from Mexico, but today you'll find me in NYC studying language and expanding my knowledge of contemporary design.

“I believe design has to be more than visually appealing - it has to lend itself to functional solutions. I love to tackle new challenges and believe it's important to learn new things every day.”

More than simply a visual art, I would describe Industrial Design as a plan for arranging elements to accomplish a particular purpose. It is a field of design that demands an aesthetic eye as well as an understanding of functionality.

In my work experience as a Structural Designer in Mexico, I was responsible for developing packaging that was not only aesthetically appealing, but functional, protective, and user-friendly. Ensuring these ergonomic factors are properly addressed, while innovating on the packaging design was my strong suit, and I enjoyed the daily challenges of this role.

My studies as UX Designer at Columbia University NY have complemented and expanded my knowledge of this field by training me to properly research and develop an in-depth understanding the user's needs at the onset of every project. This knowledge and understanding is then used to drive every aspect of the project.

As UI Designer, I have been learning how to create interactive programs that enhance a customer's experience with a brand and facilitate an enjoyable experience on the business's website.

My goals are to have the opportunity to expand my work experience, put in practice the coursework I have learned, acquire knowledge, as well as develop new job skills.
